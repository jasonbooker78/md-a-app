# MD Anderson Design System Instructions

## 1. Color Palette
- **Primary Red:** Used for headers, call-to-action buttons, and important highlights.
- **Accent Purple:** Used for secondary callouts and backgrounds.
- **Black/White/Gray:** For text, backgrounds, and section separation.
- **Blue:** Used for links and some icons.

## 2. Typography
- **Headings:** Use 'Minion Regular', 'Minion W01 Cap Regular', Arial, Georgia. Large, bold, and prominent—white on dark backgrounds when needed.
- **Body Text & UI:** Use "Univers LT W01_65 Bold" for all body copy and UI elements. Black or dark gray on white/light backgrounds for readability.
- **Subheadings/Labels:** Smaller, uppercase, or bold for emphasis, using the body font.

## 3. Layout & Spacing
- **Full-Width Hero:** Large hero section with headline, supporting text, and call-to-action.
- **Grid System:** Use a simple grid for cards and highlights (e.g., 2-4 columns depending on section).
- **Section Padding:** Generous vertical spacing between sections for clarity.
- **Panels/Cards:** Use cards with subtle shadows and colored top borders for grouping content.

## 4. Buttons & Links
- **Primary Buttons:** Solid red background, white text, rectangular with slight rounding.
- **Secondary Buttons:** Outlined or filled with accent colors (purple, blue, black).
- **Links:** Underlined or colored blue for visibility.

## 5. Iconography
- **Simple Line Icons:** Used for navigation, actions, and section highlights.
- **Consistent Style:** All icons should be of similar weight and style.

## 6. Navigation
- **Top Navigation Bar:** Fixed at the top, includes logo, main links, and utility actions (search, language, etc.).
- **Section Navigation:** Use colored bars or tabs for section switching.

## 7. Cards & Highlights
- **Highlight Cards:** Use colored backgrounds and icons for emphasis (e.g., hospital ranking, connection programs).
- **Content Cards:** Image or icon, title, and short description. Use grid layout for multiple cards.

## 8. Forms & Search
- **Search Bars:** Prominent, with clear placeholder text and search icon.
- **Form Fields:** Simple, rectangular, with clear labels and sufficient spacing.

## 9. Footer
- **Multi-Column Layout:** Organized links, contact info, and social media.
- **Legal & Branding:** Placed at the very bottom, small font size.

---

> **Tip:** Maintain high contrast for accessibility. Use consistent spacing, font sizes, and color usage throughout the site.
